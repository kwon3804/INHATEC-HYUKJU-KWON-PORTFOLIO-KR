02.

THE POWER TO MAKE THE WORLD ROLL

RECYCLING WITH SHARED MOBILITY

DESIGN PROCESS

A DESIGN THAT FITS THE CITY'S AESTHETIC

The aging bicycle storage has been left unattended for years or months, so it can no longer be used for original storage purposes. Accordingly, the government has invested a lot of budget to carry out reorganization projects, but over time, it has no choice but to return to a neglected state.
Therefore, the bicycle battery relay station was created by combining energy recycling with a shared mobility business that everyone can own, not individual ownership.

04.

SHADOW IN THE GREAT GLORY

OPERATION CHROMITE EXHIBITION AND MEMORIAL HALL

DESIGN PROCESS

WHEN THERE IS LIGHT, THERE IS A SHADOW

It explains the concept and development stage of the landing operation in the Korean War, the meaning of the landing operation, and establishes a memorial space to find out about Wolmido Island, which was damaged by the U.S. bombing during the Korean War, and to commemorate the innocent victims.
The exhibition hall is composed of high light and darkened U.S. military bombings and memorial spaces that created innocent victims, further deepening the exhibition hall.
